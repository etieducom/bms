<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user, representing a training institute named ETI Educom, requested a full-fledged lead management and institute management system.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
-   **Lead Management:**
    -   Capture leads with details: name, numbers, address, email, program, fee, payment plan, and source (which should be definable by the super admin).
    -   Manage lead statuses and track follow-ups.
    -   Soft-delete leads with a reason, viewable in a Deleted Leads section. Only a Branch Admin can delete a lead from their branch.
-   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):**
    -   **Super Admin:** Full access. Manages branches, programs, users, expense categories, and lead sources. Views aggregated cross-branch analytics. Manages WhatsApp notification settings.
    -   **Branch Admin:** Manages a single branch. Can view all data for their branch, manage leads (including deletion), manage expenses, and view branch-specific reports. Cannot see an Admin Panel.
    -   **Counsellor:** Can add/manage leads and follow-ups for their assigned branch. Cannot delete leads.
    -   **Front Desk Executive (FDE):** Manages expenses and enrollments/payments for their branch.
-   **User and Branch Management:** Admin can perform full CRUD on branches and users.
-   **Program & Source Management:** Admin can perform full CRUD on programs and lead sources.
-   **Enrollment and Payments:**
    -   FDE enrolls converted leads.
    -   System handles one-time and installment payments, preventing over-payment.
    -   Generate and print receipts for each payment.
    -   Dedicated pages for viewing all payments and pending installment payments with comprehensive filters.
-   **Expense Management:** FDEs record branch expenses; Admins define categories, and Branch Admins can delete expenses for their branch.
-   **Dashboard & Analytics:**
    -   **Super Admin:** Branch-wise comparison dashboard (leads, enrollments, income) to identify performance.
    -   **Branch Admin:** Dashboard showing key metrics for their own branch.
-   **Resource Sharing:** A central repository where Super Admin can upload marketing/learning materials (brochures, videos, etc.) for all users to access.
-   **WhatsApp Integration (MSG91):**
    -   System to send automated notifications for: lead creation, demo booking, demo completion, enrollment confirmation, and payment receipts.
    -   Send payment reminders for upcoming installments.
    -   Super Admin can enable/disable these notifications globally.

**User's preferred language**: English
